About Me

I'm a Spatial Analyst and Geomatics Engineer with a strong interest in spatial analysis, geospatial technologies, and their applications in disaster management. I enjoy exploring how geospatial information can help us better understand complex challenges and support more effective planning, preparedness, and response. My passion lies in using geospatial technologies to uncover patterns, understand risks, and support better decision-making.


Currently, I work as a Disaster Data Analyst at Indonesia's National Agency for Disaster Management (BNPB), analysing and communicating disaster related-information through maps, visualisations, reports to support decision-making. As a Geomatics Engineer with experience in the surveying and mapping industry, I have led data processing teams responsible for delivering high-accuracy topographic mapping products, including aerial photogrammetry, LiDAR processing, multi-source spatial data integration, and map quality assessment.
